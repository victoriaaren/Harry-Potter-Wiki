Unicorn

Species Facts Book(s) Mentioned Image
  1. Hair color: changes based on age (gold in first two years, silver in adolescence, white when matured)
  2. Friendly towards witches and wizards
  3. Physical traits: one horn on its head, white fur, golden hooves
  4. Its blood has the magic ability to keep one alive, but anyone who slays a unicorn will be cursed for harming such a pure creature
  5. Its horn and hair have magical properties also
  6. Ministry of Magic Classification: BEAST XXXX
