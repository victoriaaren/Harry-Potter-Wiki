Hippogriff

Species Facts Book(s) Mentioned Image
  1. A grandly magical winged beast with animal features such as: front legs, wings and head of an eagle, and a horse body, tail and hind legs
  2. Proper human etiquette must be shown to avoid being in danger, since Hippogriffs are extremely prideful (ex. bowing, maintaining eye contact)
  3. Tamable and extremely loyal and protective of the humans they trust
  4. Ministry of Magic Classification: Beast XXX
