House Elf (Elf)

Species Facts Book(s) Mentioned Image
  1. Known for their wandless house-elf magic - a unique and powerful kind of magic allowing them to complete tasks such as Apparation
  2. Size: 3 ft
  3. Tend to be very short and thin with large pointed floppy ears
  4. Extremely loyal and dedicated to their master (they usually serve witches and wizards) and will obey every command until they are freed
  5. In order to free a house-elf, you must present to them an item of clothing
  6. Often mistreated (sad)
  7. Ministry of Magic Classification: Being
